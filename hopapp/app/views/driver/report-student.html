STUDENT BEHAVIOR REPORT

Policy

In accordance with Policy 804; student conduct on the school buses is to be consistent with that expected of the student in the classroom. Violations or misbebehavior may result in the suspension of privileges. This form is to be used as a communication tool between bus drivers, school principals and the transportation services office to awareness of unacceptable behavior of students while being transported to and from school.
